Egg On Toast Recipe

Perfectly fried egg on toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • 1 or two slices of bread
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan and add the egg/s
  2. Put the toast in the toaster and pull down slider
  3. Fry the egg for several minutes, splash oil over the top after the egg white has gone white
  4. Butter the toast once its golden brown and put the egg/s top
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper then serve
