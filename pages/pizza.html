Frozen Pizza

Frozen Pizza Recipe

Sick in the oven, come back in twenty. Boom!

Ingredients

  • One frozen pizza

Instructions

  1. Take the pizza out the box. Bonus: Cut the box open so it looks like a takeaway pizza box and use instead of a plate
  2. Put the pizza in the oven on the shelf it says to on the box
  3. Cook for the time and temperature it says to on the box
